Launch Strategy Overview

The sequencing is designed to build proof-of-concept before scaling spend, ensuring every rupee deployed after Week 12 is informed by real market data.

Amazon India — Primary E-Commerce Channel (35% Revenue Target)

Amazon is the primary discovery and conversion channel for the first 6 months. The Sugar Substitutes category has moderate competition with Sugar Free (Zydus) dominating, but the prebiotic positioning creates a unique lane.

Account Setup & Brand Registry

  • Seller Account: Register as Professional Seller (₹499/month). Required: GST certificate, PAN, bank account, phone number, email
  • GTIN/EAN Barcodes: Purchase from GS1 India (₹4,500 for 10 barcodes). One per SKU. Apply to packaging before production
  • Brand Registry: Apply immediately with trademark application number (takes 2-4 weeks). Enables A+ Content, Brand Store, Brand Analytics
  • FSSAI License: Upload to seller profile. Required for food category listing. License number must be on all packaging
  • Category Approval: "Diet & Nutrition" category may require additional approval. Prepare test reports, FSSAI docs, product images in advance

Listing Optimization

  • Title Formula (200 chars max): [Brand] + [Product] + [Key Benefit] + [Size] + [Use Case]. Example: "[Brand Name] Prebiotic FOS Sweetener | Natural Sugar Alternative | Gut Health + Zero Glycemic | 250g Jar | For Diabetics, Fitness & Weight Management"
  • 5 Bullet Points:
    1. PREBIOTIC GUT HEALTH BENEFIT: Only sweetener that feeds beneficial gut bacteria. Patented FOS technology (US10167459B2)
    2. ZERO GLYCEMIC INDEX: Safe for diabetics. Does not spike blood sugar. Clinically studied Fructooligosaccharides
    3. NATURAL & CLEAN LABEL: No artificial chemicals, no sucralose, no aspartame. Plant-derived prebiotic fiber. FSSAI approved
    4. VERSATILE USAGE: Use in tea, coffee, smoothies, baking, cooking. Heat stable. 1 teaspoon = sweetness of 1.5 tsp sugar
    5. MADE IN INDIA: By Revelations Biotech, Hyderabad. cGMP certified facility. Quality tested at every batch
  • A+ Content Modules: 7 modules — Hero banner, comparison chart (vs Sugar Free, Stevia, Splenda), benefits infographic, usage guide, science explainer, testimonials, brand story
  • Backend Keywords (250 bytes max): sugar substitute, sugar free, diabetic sweetener, prebiotic, FOS, fructooligosaccharide, gut health sweetener, natural sweetener, keto sweetener, low calorie sweetener, zero glycemic sweetener, healthy sugar alternative

Blinkit Onboarding — Step by Step

  1. Seller Registration: Submit form at Blinkit seller portal with company details, GSTIN, PAN, FSSAI license
  2. Document Verification: 7-10 business days. Ensure all documents are clear and current
  3. Category Manager Assignment: Once verified, a Blinkit category manager reviews your portfolio
  4. Product Listing Upload: Submit catalog with images, descriptions, MRP, barcodes. Must pass compliance check
  5. APOB Setup: Register Additional Place of Business (Blinkit warehouse address) under your GST. Critical step
  6. PLA Payment: Pay ₹25,000 per SKU per cluster. Start with 2 SKUs (250g + sachets) in 1 cluster (Hyderabad)
  7. Review Period: 7-15 days for final review and go-live approval
  8. First PO: Blinkit generates purchase order. Dispatch to designated dark stores within 48hrs

Dark Store Operations

  • Inventory per dark store: Typical MOQ is 24-48 units per SKU per dark store
  • Replenishment cycle: Weekly. Monitor sell-through rates and replenish proactively
  • Pricing: MRP is selling price. Platform takes 25-35% margin. You receive 65-75% of MRP
  • Example (250g at ₹449 MRP): Platform margin ~30% (₹135) → You receive ~₹314 → COGS ₹120 → Gross margin ~₹194 (43%)

Quick Commerce Onboarding — Step-by-Step

Blinkit (Zomato)

Market share: ~50% of India quick commerce (Sep 2025)

Onboarding steps:

  1. Apply at blinkit.com/sell-on-blinkit
  2. Submit: GSTIN, PAN, bank a/c, FSSAI license, product catalog with MRP
  3. Category team review (3-7 business days)
  4. If approved: Ship bulk inventory to assigned dark stores
  5. Listing goes live. Blinkit handles fulfillment + delivery

Commission: Dynamic by category. Health foods typically 15-25%

Min order value: No per-order fulfillment for sellers

Timeline: 2-4 weeks end-to-end

Zepto

Market share: ~25% (fastest growing)

Onboarding steps:

  1. Apply at zepto.co/brands or email brands@zeptonow.com
  2. Submit: GSTIN, FSSAI, product details, MRP, margin structure
  3. Category manager call (1-2 weeks)
  4. Negotiate commission and promotional slots
  5. Ship inventory to Zepto fulfillment centers

Commission: Category-based + possible onboarding fee

Ad platform: Zepto Ads (Sponsored listings from ₹2-5 CPC)

Timeline: 3-5 weeks

Swiggy Instamart

Market share: ~20%

Onboarding steps:

  1. Contact: partner.instamart@swiggy.in
  2. Submit brand deck, FSSAI, product catalog
  3. Category approval + pricing negotiation
  4. Inventory shipment to Instamart dark stores
  5. Go live with listing + Swiggy Ads

Commission: 18-25% (negotiable for new brands)

Promotional: Banner ads, category takeovers available

Timeline: 3-6 weeks
