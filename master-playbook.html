FOS Formulation Specifications

Active Ingredient
  • FOS Purity: 95% minimum (sc-FOS preferred)
  • Source: Enzymatic conversion from sucrose
  • Caloric Value: 2.01 kcal/g (vs sugar 4.0 kcal/g)
  • Glycemic Index: <20 (ultra-low)

  • FOS base (95% purity) for sweetness + prebiotic function
  • Taste optimization for 1:1 sugar replacement
  • Flow agent (silicon dioxide, 0.5-1%) for powder pourability
  • No artificial sweeteners, no sugar alcohols, no maltodextrin
  • Patented technology (India, USA, Russia patents)
