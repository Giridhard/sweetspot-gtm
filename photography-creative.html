7 Shots
Shot 1 of 7: Hero Shot HERO

Purpose: Primary product image for e-commerce listings, website hero, press kit. Must convey premium quality, warmth, and trust.

Camera Angle
45 degrees front-right elevation. Camera positioned at product mid-height, angled slightly down to show jar lid and label simultaneously. Tripod-locked.
Lens & Settings
100mm macro (Canon EF 100mm f/2.8L or Sigma 105mm). Aperture: f/4 for full-product sharpness. ISO 100. Shutter: 1/125s (strobe sync). Manual focus on label text center.
Key Light
Strobe with 60x90cm softbox, positioned 45 degrees above-left at 1.5m distance. Power: 100% (guide number matched to f/4 at ISO 100). Creates defined shadow on right for dimension.
Fill Light
Second strobe with 90cm octabox, positioned camera-right at 30% power. Ratio: 3:1 key-to-fill. Softens shadows without eliminating them — maintains premium dimensionality.
Bounce / Reflector
White foam-core bounce card placed 30cm below product surface, angled 15 degrees upward. Opens up under-lid shadow. Optional: gold reflector card for warm underlight on glass jar body.
Background
Cream seamless paper roll (#FFF8E7). Roll extends from background wall to table surface for infinity curve. No visible horizon line. Paper must be wrinkle-free and dust-free.
Props
None. Product only. Jar must be fingerprint-free (lint-free gloves during handling). Label must face camera dead-center. Lid on, slightly angled for visual interest.
Post-Production
Clean background to pure cream. Remove any dust/scratches on glass. Enhance gold label shimmer. Slight warm color grade (+5 warmth, -10 saturation). Add subtle reflection on surface. Deliver with and without shadow.

Post-Production Specifications

Color Grading & Editing Standards
Color Temperature
Target: 5400K warmth across all images. +5 warmth shift in Lightroom/Capture One. Consistent across entire shoot — all images must feel like the same brand world.
Saturation
Global: -10 saturation for sophisticated, non-garish feel. Exception: product gold color boosted +15 (vibrance, not saturation) to pop.
Tone Curve
Lifted blacks (shadows raised to 10/255). Slightly compressed highlights (-15). Creates a “film” look without being hipster. Maintains detail in shadows and highlights.
Grain
Subtle film grain: Amount 15, Size 25, Roughness 50 in Lightroom. Adds organic texture, reduces digital-clinical feel. Consistent grain across all images.
Retouching
Product: remove dust, fingerprints, scratches, label imperfections. Do NOT remove glass reflections (they add realism). Skin retouching on models: frequency separation for blemishes only — no porcelain skin, no heavy retouching. Must look natural.
Delivery Formats
Per shot: RAW (archival), Edited TIFF (16-bit, Adobe RGB, master), JPEG (sRGB, quality 95, web), PNG (transparent bg, sRGB), WebP (optimized, 80% quality for web speed). Named per convention in Section 10.
