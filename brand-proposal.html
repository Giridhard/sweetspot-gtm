Prebiotic Sugar Replacer Brand

Brand naming, identity, and initial investment proposal for the sc-FOS consumer brand — from Inspire Labs to Revelations Biotech.

Prepared by Inspire Labs Date May 2026
01 · Context
The Opportunity
Revelations Biotech manufactures sc-FOS — a prebiotic fiber that tastes like sugar, feeds gut bacteria, and costs 28.6% less than the nearest branded competitor. No consumer brand exists for it yet. This document covers three decisions needed before anything else moves: what to name the brand, what it should look and feel like, and what the initial investment is to bring it to life.

03 · Brand Identity

Typography Direction

Headlines

Playfair Display

Serif. Elegant, editorial, premium. Signals heritage and credibility. Used for headlines, hero sections, and packaging feature text. Communicates "this is not a cheap substitute."

Body & UI

Inter

Clean sans-serif. Optimized for screens. Used for body text, product descriptions, website UI, and all digital surfaces. Highly readable at small sizes — critical for nutritional info and packaging fine print.

Data & Labels

JetBrains Mono

Monospace. For nutritional data, lab results, clinical numbers, and metadata. Signals scientific precision and transparency. "We show the numbers because we're proud of them."

06 · Next Steps
Three Decisions Needed
Once these are resolved, Inspire Labs can begin Engine A execution within 5 business days.
Decision 1

Brand Name

Review the 12 candidates. Shortlist top 3 for trademark search (₹15K, 5 days). Final selection after TM clearance. Inspire Labs will handle domain acquisition and social handle reservation in parallel.

Decision 2

Identity Direction

Approve or refine the visual direction — color palette, typography, packaging concept. This becomes the brief for the design team. Refinements are expected; this is a starting point, not a final.

Decision 3

Engine A Go/No-Go

Approve the ₹1.40Cr initial investment to begin brand foundation. Capital deployment: ₹40L at signing, ₹50L at Week 4 (design phase), ₹50L at Week 10 (production phase).
