📖 Section 9: Category Creation Playbook

5 Universal Patterns for Category Creation

1. Start with a Wedge

Diabetics who already buy Sugar Free — not the mass market. They have the pain, the purchase behavior, and the budget. Convert them first.

2. Build Community Before Scaling

WhatsApp groups for diabetic support, recipe sharing, gut health tips. Community creates retention and word-of-mouth that no ad budget can match.

3. Solve the Trust Problem

Doctor endorsements, clinical studies, FSSAI compliance. In health, trust precedes trial. No amount of performance marketing overcomes trust deficit.

4. Get Unit Economics Right

Powder format has better margins than liquid. Launch powder ASAP. Sachets enable trial at Rs 99 (lower barrier to first purchase).

5. Adapt to Local Context

Indian dessert recipes, chai/coffee use cases, festival tie-ins (Diwali mithai). Cultural relevance drives adoption faster than clinical data.
