Regulatory & Certification Requirements — UAE

  • Renewal: Annual audit + documentation review

  • Product registration: Each SKU must be registered with municipality
  • Labeling: Arabic + English required, nutrition panel, ingredients

  • Health claims: Must be approved — prebiotic claims need substantiation
  • Free zone advantage: JAFZA, DAFZA offer streamlined import processes
  • Timeline: 6-10 weeks from application to shelf

FDA GRAS Pathway for FOS

FOS is already GRAS-approved by the FDA since 2000. Short-chain FOS (polymerization degree 3-5) is Generally Recognized As Safe for use as a general-purpose food ingredient and bulking agent. Safe up to 20g/day for adults. Also permitted in infant formulas.

GRAS Status Details

  • GRAS Notice 623: FDA formal acknowledgment for FOS
  • Classification: General-purpose food ingredient & bulking agent
  • Permitted use: Various food categories including beverages, baked goods, dairy
  • Safety level: Up to 20g/day established as safe for adults
  • Infant formula: Also approved — reflecting high safety confidence
  • EFSA approval: Also approved in EU — supports UK market entry

What This Means for [Brand Name]

  • No new GRAS filing needed: Leverage existing FOS GRAS status
  • Food classification preferred: Sell as food ingredient, not dietary supplement
  • Labeling: Can list "Fructooligosaccharides" or "FOS (Prebiotic Fiber)" on label
  • Health claims: Structure/function claims allowed (prebiotic, gut health)
  • No disease claims: Cannot claim to treat/prevent diabetes
  • Timeline advantage: Skip 12-18 month GRAS review — immediate market entry

US Labeling & Classification Strategy

Food Classification (Recommended)

  • Classification: Food ingredient / sweetener — NOT dietary supplement
  • Reason: Broader retail distribution, simpler compliance, consumer trust
  • Label: "Nutrition Facts" panel (not "Supplement Facts")
  • Permitted claims: Structure/function claims with disclaimer
  • Example claim: "Contains prebiotic fiber that supports digestive health*"
  • Disclaimer required: "*This statement has not been evaluated by the FDA..."

Key Label Requirements

  • Product name: "Prebiotic Sweetener" or "FOS Natural Sweetener"
  • Nutrition Facts: Calories, total carbs, dietary fiber, added sugars
  • Serving size: In US customary units (teaspoons, grams)
  • Allergen declaration: Required even if no allergens present
  • Country of origin: "Product of India" required
  • Net weight: US customary + metric (oz and grams)

UK Sugar Tax (Soft Drinks Industry Levy)

How It Works

  • Lower band: 18p/litre for drinks with 5-8g sugar per 100ml
  • Higher band: 24p/litre for drinks with 8g+ sugar per 100ml
  • Impact: 50%+ reduction in sugar content of soft drinks since 2018
  • Revenue: Generates ~£300M/year for government
