Key Insight: The Critical Reorder Window

Days 15-45 After First Purchase = Make or Break. Research from Indian D2C analytics firms shows that the critical window for health food and supplement brands is days 15-45 after first purchase. This is when most customers decide whether to reorder. Brands that pair subscription offers with educational email sequences (explaining when users should expect to feel results, sharing usage tips, surfacing social proof) see significantly lower first-refill churn. For [Brand Name] FOS, this means the reorder nudge must land between Day 25-30 when a 100g pack would be running low.

The Subscription Advantage: LTV Multiplier

Subscription customers generate 200-300% higher CLV than one-time buyers. The average subscription customer stays 6-12 months versus 1-2 purchases for non-subscribers. For a D2C brand with a monthly subscription value of ₹1,500, a subscriber who stays 8 months generates ₹12,000 CLV compared to ₹2,000-₹3,000 from a typical non-subscriber.

2.5 Pause/Skip/Swap Flexibility

Churn reduction impact: Industry data shows that offering pause/skip/swap options reduces subscription churn by 15-20%. The alternative to pausing is canceling -- and once a customer cancels, the win-back rate is only 5-8% vs. 70-80% resume rate for paused subscriptions.

Pause

  • Pause for 1, 2, or 3 months
  • Auto-resume after pause period
  • Keep subscription discount
  • Reminder before resume
  • Target: 10% of active subs use pause/quarter

Skip

  • Skip next delivery only
  • One-click skip via email/SMS
  • No penalty, maintains pricing
  • "Skip" button in every pre-shipment email
  • Target: 15% of deliveries skipped

Swap

  • Swap to different SKU (100g, 250g, 500g)
  • Change frequency anytime
  • Swap to bundle or gift pack
  • Price adjusts automatically
  • Target: 8% of orders involve swap

  • 50 bonus points for account creation

  • 10 points for following on Instagram
  • 15 points for sharing a recipe on social

Redemption Value

4.6 Anti-Fraud Measures

Fraud Prevention Rules

  • One referral per household: Same shipping address = same household; no self-referral
  • Phone number verification: OTP on new account; unique phone per referral
  • Minimum order value: Referee must place order of ₹299+ for referral to count
  • Return window: Referral credit awarded only after 14-day return window expires
  • IP/device tracking: Flag referrals from same IP or device fingerprint
  • Velocity limits: Maximum 10 successful referrals per month per referrer
  • Credit vs cash: Referral rewards are store credit only, not cashable
  • Manual review: Auto-flag patterns >5 referrals/day for manual review

Common Fraud Patterns to Watch

  • Self-referral with multiple email addresses
  • "Referral farms" -- creating fake accounts to earn credits
  • Buying then returning to earn referral credit
  • Sharing on coupon/deal sites (dilutes exclusivity)
  • VPN/proxy use to bypass IP restrictions

Response: Automated flagging system + manual review for outliers. Accounts with suspicious patterns get referral credits frozen pending review.
