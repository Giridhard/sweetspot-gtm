01 HoReCa Market Overview & Opportunity Sizing

India's HoReCa Industry — Scale & Growth

India's HoReCa (Hotels, Restaurants, Catering) market reached an estimated USD 267.43 billion in 2025, projected to grow at a CAGR of 6.8% to reach USD 520 billion by 2035. The foodservice market alone stands at USD 85.19 billion (2025), expanding at 10.3% CAGR to reach USD 153.37 billion by 2031. This represents one of the world's fastest-growing hospitality markets, fueled by urbanization, rising disposable incomes, and a cultural shift toward dining out.
